Early-stage projects lose trust when they present future ambitions as if they were already institutional facts. Karyon has a strong theory surface and a real codebase, but it is still in the stage where careful framing matters as much as breadth.

That is why the public site is intentionally narrow: fewer sections, more explicit status, and more links into real work.
